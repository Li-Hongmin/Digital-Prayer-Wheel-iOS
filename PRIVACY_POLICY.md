## English Version

### Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how "è‡³è¯šè½¬ç»è½®" (Digital Prayer Wheel) ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS, iPadOS, and watchOS applications.

### Information We Collect

**We do NOT collect any personal information.**

The App is designed with privacy in mind:
- **No user accounts required** - You can use all features without registration
- **No network connections** - The App works completely offline
- **No analytics or tracking** - We do not track your usage or behavior
- **No third-party services** - No external SDKs or services are integrated

### Data Storage

All data is stored **locally on your device only**:

1. **Prayer Counts**: Your prayer recitation counts are saved using iOS UserDefaults
2. **Settings**: Your preference settings (display mode, speed, opacity, etc.) are stored locally
3. **Daily Statistics**: Daily merit counts and consecutive days are stored on your device
4. **Background Calculation Data**: Timestamp and rotation speed for the intelligent catch-up system

**Apple Watch Data Sync**:
- If you use the Apple Watch version, data is synchronized between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's Watch Connectivity framework
- This sync happens **only between your devices** - no data is sent to external servers

### Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports (unless you choose to share with Apple)
- Contact information
- Photos or camera access
- Microphone or audio data

### Data Sharing

**We do NOT share any data with third parties** because we don't collect any data in the first place.

Your prayer counts and settings remain **private and on your device**.

### Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages (4+) and does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

### Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Your data is protected by your device's security measures (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
- We recommend enabling device encryption and using a strong passcode
- Regular backups through iCloud or iTunes will include your prayer counts

### Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
- **Access**: View all your data within the App
- **Delete**: Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data
- **Export**: You can view statistics within the App (no export feature currently)

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

### Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
- **GitHub Issues**: https://github.com/Li-Hongmin/Digital-Prayer-Wheel-iOS/issues
- **Project URL**: https://github.com/Li-Hongmin/Digital-Prayer-Wheel-iOS
